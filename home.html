How It Works

Three steps. Zero effort.

Give SmartDocs a video, and it handles everything else. No editing skills required.

01

Upload Your Video

Drag and drop any video file or paste a YouTube link. SmartDocs accepts workshops, webinars, tutorials, screen recordings — any format, any length.

02

AI Does the Work

SmartDocs transcribes the audio, identifies every distinct lesson or topic, extracts short clips, captures key screenshots, and writes clear step-by-step instructions.

Get Your Guides

Get organized, illustrated SOP documents for each topic. Each guide comes with a short video clip, annotated screenshots, and numbered steps anyone can follow.
